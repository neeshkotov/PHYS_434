Problem 5
if X has mean = mu1, variance = var1
and Y has mean = mu2, variance = var2
then Z = X+Y has mean = mu1+mu2, variance = var1+var2
the standard deviation being sqrt(variance) is sqrt(var1+var2) which is sqrt(sigma1^2+sigma2^2)
